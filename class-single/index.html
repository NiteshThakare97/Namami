About Us

Experience comes with practice

SENSAI VILAS BOBDE (7th Degree Black Belt, Jeet Kune Do (JKD) style ,Martial art)

For the past 35 years Sensai Vilas Bobde is teaching the following subjects viz-Martial Arts ,Gymnastics, Karate, Kung Fu, Weapons (Nun Chucks, Stick, Sword, Tonfa), Self Defence, Street Fight and defence against weapons.

Sensai Vilas Bobde is also founder of the organization- “National Association of Martial Arts Masters India (NAMAMI)”. Sensai Vilas Bobde has taught nearly 2000 officers of IPS, IRS, Customs & IAS cadres in National Academy of Direct Taxes (NADT) at Nagpur. He had also taught Clerks, Officers and Cashiers of Punjab National Bank in Self Defence Technique and 500 Guards of Coal fields in Nagpur.

Everyone must choose one of two pains: The pain of discipline or the pain of regret.

Jim Rohn

He also has taken 20 National Tournaments & 52 Camps of Martial Art training across various cities in INDIA. Till date Sensai Vilas Bobde has been honoured with more than 200 Awards & prizes for his exellency in the field of Martial Arts. In 2013 Sensai Vilas Bobde was awarded with SANDESH AWARD for teaching 25000 GIRLS in Self Defence technique at Reshimbagh ground, Nagpur.

Under his visionary leadership, the National Association of Martial Arts Masters India (NAMAMI) has flourished as a beacon of martial arts education, promoting values of discipline, respect, and physical fitness. Sensai Vilas Bobde's influence extends beyond the dojo, as he continues to inspire and shape the lives of thousands of individuals, instilling in them the principles of self-defense, confidence, and a holistic approach to personal well-being.

SENSAI VIHANG BOBDE (3rd Degree Dan Black Belt, Jeet Kune Do (JKD) style ,Martial art)

Continuing our journey of dedication to martial arts excellence, Sensai Vihang Bobde has extended his influence, fostering the growth and development of martial arts practitioners. With a profound commitment to imparting knowledge and instilling discipline, he has conducted various workshops and seminars, leaving an indelible mark on the global martial arts community.

In addition to his extensive teaching experience, Sensai Vihang Bobde continues to lead by example, exemplifying the true spirit of a martial arts master. His dedication to the advancement of martial arts education, coupled with his unwavering commitment to community service, distinguishes him as a luminary in the world of martial arts. As we look ahead, Sensai Vihang Bobde remains steadfast in his mission to shape future generations of martial artists and contribute to the global legacy of martial arts excellence.

1. What types of courses do you offer?

We provide a diverse range of martial arts courses, including Karate, Kick boxing, J.K.D style of Bruce Lee, Gymnastics, Weapons training, Self-Defense, Street Fight techniques, and defense against weapons. Our comprehensive curriculum is designed to cater to various interests and skill levels.

2. How long does each course last, and what is the session frequency?

It is a 1 year course which is divided into 4 session duration of 3 months each. Typically, courses are structured with regular weekly sessions, ranging from 1 to 2 hours each. Our instructors will provide detailed information about the duration and frequency for each course.

3. Do you offer certification upon course completion?

Yes, we provide certifications upon successful completion of our courses. These certifications are recognised by 1) Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports (Govt. of India) 2) Indian Olympic Association 3) School Games Federation of India.
